# Root Cause Analysis Report
## Method: 5 Whys | Author: Bhavana Gurajala | Date: 2025-12-15

---

## Problem Statement
Operational reports were being delivered 3-5 days late every month,
causing delayed decision-making at the management level.

---

## 5 Whys Analysis

**Why 1:** Why were reports delivered late?
Because data collection from multiple teams took too long.

**Why 2:** Why did data collection take too long?
Because each team used a different format and submitted data manually via email.

**Why 3:** Why were teams using different formats?
Because there was no standardized reporting template in place.

**Why 4:** Why was there no standardized template?
Because no one had formally documented or enforced a reporting process.

**Why 5:** Why was there no documented process?
Because reporting requirements had never been formally gathered or signed off by stakeholders.

---

## Root Cause
Lack of formalized business requirements and standardized reporting process.

---

## Corrective Actions
| Action | Owner | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Create standardized reporting template | Business Analyst | Week 1 |
| Document reporting requirements in BRD | Business Analyst | Week 2 |
| Migrate all reporting to centralized dashboard | Data Team | Week 3-4 |
| Schedule monthly stakeholder review meetings | Operations Manager | Ongoing |

## Result
After implementing corrective actions, report delivery time reduced from
5 days to same-day automated delivery via Power BI dashboard.
